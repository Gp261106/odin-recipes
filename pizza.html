The Greatest Pepperoni pizza ever my brobro

    Ingredients
  • 100 gr cottage cheese
  • 250gr tomato sauce
  • 200gr Pepperoni
  • Pre cooked bread base
    Steps
  1. Pre heat the oven for 15 minutes 150 degrees
  2. In the brad base put the tomato sauce, the cottage cheese and the Pepperoni
  3. Let the Pizza cooked for around 30 minutes 200 degrees
  4. Put Gloves on and take the pizza out of the oven
