Scramble eggs

The Easiest way to prepare your eggs

    Ingredients
  • 3 eggs
  • 50 ml Milk
  • 200gr sausages(this is optional)
      • Steps
    1. Scramble all the eggs togheter 20 times
    2. pre heat the pan for a couple minutes then put the eggs inside the pan
    3. let them cooked therer for a minute and then add the milk
    4. Scramble the eggs with the milk and take them out the heat once you can see there is no raw eggs
