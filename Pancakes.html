Pancakes

The most Delicious pancakes on the web way to prepare your eggs

    Ingredients
  • 1 egg
  • 100 ml Milk
  • 400gr all purpose flour
      • Steps
    1. mix the egg with the milk until they are totally combined
    2. add the flour to the mix
    3. let them cooked until you are able to see bubbles then cooked the other side
    4. take them out the pan, enjoy with a bit of honey
